World Cultures

Welcome to this Big Data solution to leverage Every Culture Encylopedia to learn about all cultures existing in the world.

https://www.everyculture.com/

The purpose of this research project is to do some data analytics to summarize important data like how many cultures exists in the world, per country, and continents.
At a later phase the objective is to learn things that are commong among cultures. Likewise, what food they eat, to answer the common saying "That you are what you Eat".
Also, we can partner with the Encylopedia owner to help maintaining the info current and accurate. Like providing a better view to see where there is missing data.
